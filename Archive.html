Having an issue?

If your're stuck at this view, something is wrong in the archive.

  • Try to run the extension again in browser, it may auto fix the archive.
  • If the archive isn't immediately fixed, but download seems to be running normally, wait till the run is complete and check again.
  • You can restore the archive to a previous good state by opening your folder > data > .appdata > backups. Follow the instructions in there.
    Note: the ".appdata" folder may be hidden on Mac and Linux.
  • If nothing above works, contact the extension author:
    open the browser "console" by pressing Shift+Ctrl+J (or Command+Option+J on Mac), take a screenshot of the error messages, email to info@myfavett.com.
